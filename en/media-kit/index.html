Cosmos Week · overview for partners

This page outlines Cosmos Week’s editorial positioning, the audience the publication is trying to gather and the commercial formats that can make sense without sacrificing clarity, reputation or scientific rigor.

Updated on April 8, 2026 · commercial contact: contato@cosmosweek.com

Overview

Brand: Cosmos Week

Segment: science journalism

Core beats: astronomy, astrophysics, cosmology, space, physics and frontier research

Editorial tone: clear, critical, evidence-first and openly opposed to pseudoscience

Positioning

The project was built to publish science with context and a hierarchy of evidence. That means giving different weight to peer-reviewed papers, preprints, institutional communications and speculation. Which should be the baseline, but here we are.

Audience and fit

  • readers interested in real science, technology and education;
  • an audience highly sensitive to credibility and source quality;
  • strong fit for educational, technological and scientific brands;
  • a useful environment for reputation campaigns and qualified awareness.

Commercial formats

  • institutional banners in defined placements;
  • sponsorship of a section or thematic coverage block;
  • clearly labeled sponsorships or cultural support;
  • explicit sponsored editorials when compatible with the publication;
  • combinations across the site, social channels and editorial materials.

Acceptance criteria

Cosmos Week does not open space for pseudoscience, conspiracy narratives, miracle claims, pseudo-technical persuasion or advertising that depends on scientific ambiguity to mislead the reader.

Education, technology, astronomy, scientific culture, software, publishers, courses, events and research institutions tend to fit the project best.

Contact and next steps

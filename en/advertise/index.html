Partnerships and sponsorships

Advertise on Cosmos Week

Cosmos Week is a science journalism publication focused on astronomy, astrophysics, cosmology and frontier research. The value proposition is straightforward: editorial context, credibility and an audience that actually cares about evidence, clarity and intellectually serious reporting.

Commercial contact: contato@cosmosweek.com

Editorial profile

  • Science journalism written clearly without flattening the evidence;
  • coverage centered on astronomy, space, physics and contemporary science;
  • explicit distinction between peer-reviewed papers, preprints, hypotheses and institutional releases;
  • an environment aligned with education, technology, science and scientific culture.

Possible formats

  • institutional banners in defined areas of the site;
  • sponsorship of a section, thematic series or special package;
  • cultural support with clear identification;
  • sponsored editorial clearly labeled and never disguised;
  • packages combining the site, future newsletter and social channels.

Brands that fit the project

The best fit usually comes from publishers, courses, schools, educational platforms, technology companies, scientific software, academic institutions, events and brands connected to astronomy, science and high-value learning.

Editorial integrity

Advertising and sponsored content are always labeled. Cosmos Week does not blur the line between ads and reporting, and it does not open space for pseudoscience, obscurantism, miracle-tech claims without evidence or misleading promises dressed up as science.

How to request a proposal

Send an email with the campaign goal, preferred timing, format of interest, landing page and budget range. That speeds things up and avoids the usual five-message routine just to discover the basics.

For proposals, commercial availability and format combinations, write to contato@cosmosweek.com.

Supporting materials

The media kit summarizes editorial positioning, delivery formats and transparency criteria. When needed, updated numbers and inventory availability can be shared by email.
